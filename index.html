About Us

Crafting Pune’s Finest Spaces Since 2020

Since our inception in 2020, VISTARA INTERIORS & TEAM. Interior Designers has grown to become one of the leading & most sought after interior design companies in Pune, Maharashtra. What makes us stand out is our technical expertise, 100% customisable & modular interior design concepts with timely execution & delivery. We aim to create spaces that best suit our clients' aesthetics & needs.

The Art of Thoughtful Design

We approach each project as collaborators, not just contractors. Our process begins by listening—to your routines, your frustrations with current spaces, and your vision for what’s possible. This dialogue shapes:

  • Custom architectural details that solve real-life problems
  • Material selections balancing beauty with durability
  • Lighting strategies that adapt to moods and tasks
  • Spatial flow designed for how you move
